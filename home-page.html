Cape Town Vacations

Explore the beauty of Cape Town with Vacation Oasis

Table Mountain

Table Mountain is one of the most iconic landmarks of South Africa, offering breathtaking views of Cape Town from its flat top. Whether you're hiking or taking the cable car to the summit, this is an experience you can't miss.

Activities:

  • Hiking Trails: Various routes, from easy to challenging, to explore the mountain.
  • Cableway Rides: Enjoy a scenic cable car ride to the top with panoramic views.
  • Photography Spots: Capture the stunning landscape and city views.
  • Rock Climbing: For the more adventurous, try climbing the rock faces.
  • Nature Walks: Discover the unique flora and fauna of the mountain.

Cape Point

Cape Point is located in the Cape of Good Hope nature reserve and is known for its dramatic landscapes, towering cliffs, and rich history. It's a great spot for those looking for outdoor adventure and incredible scenery.

Activities:

  • Hiking Trails: Explore the rugged coastline and enjoy scenic views.
  • Funicular Rides: Take a ride to the top of Cape Point for incredible vistas.
  • Whale Watching: During the season, you can spot southern right whales.
  • Beaches: Visit the secluded beaches in the area.
  • Historical Lighthouse: Visit the lighthouse, which offers panoramic views of the ocean.

Robben Island

Robben Island is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is most famous for being the prison where Nelson Mandela was held for 18 years. Today, it serves as a museum and a reminder of South Africa’s history of apartheid.

Activities:

  • Guided Tours: Take a guided tour of the island and learn about its historical significance.
  • Nelson Mandela Museum: Visit the cell where Mandela was imprisoned.
  • Historical Sites: Explore the various sites and monuments on the island.
  • Boat Rides: Enjoy the scenic boat ride to and from the island.
  • Museum Visits: Learn more about South Africa's history at the Robben Island Museum.

Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden

Situated at the eastern foot of Table Mountain, Kirstenbosch is one of the world’s great botanical gardens, showcasing South Africa's diverse plant life.

Activities:

  • Garden Walks: Explore the beautifully maintained gardens.
  • Tree Canopy Walkway: Walk along the "Boomslang," a treetop walkway offering breathtaking views.
  • Picnics: Bring a picnic and relax in the beautiful natural surroundings.
  • Bird Watching: Spot native birds that frequent the garden.
  • Outdoor Concerts: Enjoy open-air summer concerts in a spectacular setting.

V&A Waterfront

The Victoria & Alfred Waterfront is Cape Town's most popular tourist destination, offering a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options along the harbor.

Activities:

  • Shopping: Explore a wide range of shops offering local and international brands.
  • Dining: Enjoy meals at one of the many waterfront restaurants.
  • Boat Cruises: Take a scenic boat cruise around the harbor or out to Robben Island.
  • Aquarium: Visit the Two Oceans Aquarium, which showcases marine life from the Atlantic and Indian Oceans.
  • Live Entertainment: Experience live music, performances, and festivals at the waterfront.

uShaka Marine World Vacations

Explore Durban's premier marine and water park with Vacation Oasis

uShaka Marine World, Durban

uShaka Marine World is one of the largest and most exciting marine-themed parks in Africa, located on Durban’s beachfront. This world-class attraction combines a marine park, water slides, dolphin shows, and a wide array of family-friendly entertainment options. It's the perfect destination for anyone looking for fun and adventure in a vibrant seaside setting.

Activities:

  • Sea World: Explore South Africa's largest aquarium, showcasing a vast variety of marine life, from sharks to colorful coral reef species.
  • Wet 'n Wild: Thrill-seekers can enjoy the exhilarating water slides, including body slides, tube rides, and a lazy river, all in one of the top water parks in South Africa.
  • Dolphin and Seal Shows: Enjoy live shows featuring talented dolphins and seals, a perfect entertainment option for families.
  • Snorkel Lagoon: Get up close with marine life as you snorkel in a lagoon filled with tropical fish and rays.
  • Dangerous Creatures Exhibit: Discover some of the world’s most dangerous and fascinating reptiles, from venomous snakes to tarantulas, in this indoor exhibit.
  • Ocean Walker: A unique underwater walking experience that lets you explore marine life from within a glass dome while walking on the sea floor.
  • Shopping & Dining: uShaka Village Walk offers various shops, restaurants, and cafés where you can enjoy local cuisine, souvenirs, and gifts.
  • Beach Activities: With its prime location right on the Durban beachfront, visitors can easily combine their uShaka experience with sunbathing, swimming, and surfing at the beach.

Top Accommodation Options near uShaka Marine World:

  • The Oyster Box: A luxurious 5-star hotel located in nearby Umhlanga, offering ocean views, world-class amenities, and proximity to both uShaka and Durban’s attractions.
  • Southern Sun Elangeni & Maharani: A popular beachfront hotel offering comfortable accommodations with easy access to the Golden Mile and uShaka Marine World.
  • Hilton Durban: A sophisticated hotel located close to Durban’s city center, providing luxury and convenience for both business and leisure travelers.
  • Protea Hotel by Marriott Durban Edward: Located on the beachfront, this hotel offers comfortable rooms and easy access to both the beach and uShaka Marine World.

Sun City Vacations

Experience luxury and adventure at South Africa’s premier resort destination with Vacation Oasis

Sun City Resort, North West Province

Sun City Resort is an extravagant entertainment complex located in the heart of the bushveld. Known for its lavish hotels, golf courses, casinos, and world-class entertainment options, Sun City is the ultimate destination for luxury seekers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Activities:

  • Valley of Waves: A thrilling water park featuring a wave pool, lazy river, and water slides suitable for all ages.
  • Golfing: Play a round of golf on one of Sun City’s prestigious golf courses, designed by legendary golfer Gary Player.
  • Game Drives: Experience the thrill of a safari with guided game drives in the nearby Pilanesberg National Park, home to the Big Five.
  • Sun City Casino: Try your luck at the casino, which offers a variety of gaming options, live entertainment, and fine dining.
  • Cultural Experiences: Engage in cultural experiences at the nearby Bakubung and Manyane resorts, which offer insights into local traditions and customs.
  • Hot Air Balloon Rides: Experience breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape with a hot air balloon ride.

Accommodation Options:

  • The Palace of the Lost City: A 5-star hotel designed to resemble an African royal palace, offering luxurious accommodations and top-notch service.
  • Cascades Hotel: A beautiful hotel surrounded by lush gardens, offering comfortable rooms and easy access to the resort's amenities.
  • Sun City Hotel: A family-friendly hotel that provides great value for money and easy access to the Valley of Waves.

Drakensberg Mountains Vacations

Experience breathtaking views and adventure with Vacation Oasis

Drakensberg Mountains, KwaZulu-Natal

The Drakensberg Mountains, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, are renowned for their stunning scenery, rich biodiversity, and a variety of outdoor activities. This region is perfect for hikers, nature lovers, and those seeking adventure amidst breathtaking landscapes.

Activities:

  • Hiking: Explore numerous hiking trails, from easy walks to challenging multi-day hikes, suitable for all skill levels.
  • Rock Climbing: Experience rock climbing opportunities on various cliff faces for adrenaline seekers.
  • Cave Painting Tours: Discover ancient San rock art, offering insights into the history and culture of indigenous people.
  • Bird Watching: Spot a variety of bird species, including the endangered Bearded Vulture.
  • Horse Riding: Experience the beauty of the mountains on horseback with guided tours available.

Accommodation Options:

  • Drakensberg Sun Resort: A family-friendly resort offering various amenities, including swimming pools, outdoor activities, and spa services.
  • Royal Natal National Park: Campsites and self-catering chalets situated within the stunning park, ideal for nature enthusiasts.
  • Cathedral Peak Hotel: A luxury hotel with stunning mountain views, offering various activities and guided hikes.

Garden Route Vacations

Explore the scenic beauty of the Garden Route with Vacation Oasis

Garden Route, Western Cape

The Garden Route is one of South Africa's most scenic drives, boasting lush forests, stunning coastlines, and charming towns. It offers numerous activities for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking for relaxation.

Activities:

  • Hiking Trails: Explore numerous hiking trails through Tsitsikamma National Park and Knysna Forest.
  • Whale Watching: Visit Hermanus for seasonal whale watching opportunities along the coast.
  • Wildlife Safaris: Experience wildlife safaris in the Garden Route National Park.
  • Beaches: Relax on some of the best beaches in the country, including Plettenberg Bay and Knysna.

Accommodation Options:

  • Plettenberg Hotel: A luxurious beachfront hotel with stunning ocean views and a range of amenities.
  • Knysna Log-Inn Hotel: A unique log cabin-style hotel located near the Knysna lagoon, offering comfortable accommodation.
  • Treetops Lodge: An eco-friendly lodge set in a tranquil forest setting.
